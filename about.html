About Us

Founded by Giri, a passionate entrepreneur from rural Andhra Pradesh, our shop blends traditional welding craftsmanship with modern techniques. We serve homeowners, farmers, small businesses, and builders who care about strong, beautiful structures.

We take pride in precision, durability, and client satisfaction. From elegant gates to reliable roofing, every job reflects our commitment to quality.

Located in Pullampeta, we’re rooted in our community—and ready to help build yours stronger.
